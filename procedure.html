"ADMISSION PROCEDURE/SELECTION CRITERIA"

STEP 1:
First fill the enquiry form and take your user id and password .Your email address is your userid and password you choose is your password. Keep your userid and password safe with you.Never share it with anyone.
STEP 2:
Check your status from clicking on the status tab.Your status shows that when you can visit the school.Dont visit without proper appointment or without any enquiry online.No action will be taken if you will do that.
STEP 3:
Your visit to the school shows that you are aware about your admission so in that case visit to the admission cell.
STEP 3:
Please carry your documents related to your admission.The following ones are required :
1. Aadhar card
2. passport size photo.
3.Previous class marksheet.
4.Tc of your previous school.
STEP 4: Also be careful about your fees because this must be submitted at the time of admission.see the fee structure of different classes in the menu bar.
STEP 5:Fee once deposited will not be refund back in case you are withdrawing the admission.
step 6:
Donot come alone without your guardian this will be taken without any response.
STEP 7:
Gaurdian aadhar card is also required.In case if your guardian is not at home come with loical guardian.
